Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my ticket?

Log in to your account, go to 'My Bookings', select the ticket and click 'Cancel'. Refunds are processed as per cancellation policy.

What payment methods are available?

We accept credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, PayPal, and wallets.

Can I change my travel date?

Date change depends on operator policy. Please contact our support team for assistance.

Will I get an SMS confirmation?

Yes, you will get your e-ticket and journey details via SMS and email after booking.
